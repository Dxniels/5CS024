Welcome to T.E.H

We are dedicated to helping the elderly in our community. Our mission is to provide support, companionship, and assistance to those who need it the most. Whether it's running errands, offering transportation, or simply spending time with them, we strive to make a positive impact in their lives. Join us in making a difference!
